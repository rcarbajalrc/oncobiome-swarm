TÍTULO DE SECCIÓN:
3.X Consistency of Model Predictions with TCGA-PAAD Clinical Data

TEXTO (Results):

To assess whether the terminal state predicted by OncoBiome Swarm —
co-depletion of NK and CD8+ compartments — is consistent with clinical
data, we analyzed RNA-seq z-scores for immune markers across 177
treatment-naive PDAC patients from TCGA-PAAD (PanCancer Atlas 2018,
paad_tcga_pan_can_atlas_2018).

We used NCAM1 (CD56) as an NK cell abundance proxy, CD8A as a CD8+
T cell proxy, and HAVCR2 (TIM-3) and PDCD1 (PD-1) as CD8+ exhaustion
markers. We note that bulk RNA-seq data from whole-tumor specimens
reflects stromal admixture and cannot capture temporal immune dynamics;
these analyses test consistency of the model's predicted terminal
immunosuppressed state, not the sequential dynamics per se.

Three findings support consistency with model predictions:

(1) NK-CD8 co-depletion enrichment. Patients in the lowest NK quartile
(NCAM1 ≤ -0.30 z-score) showed co-depletion of CD8A in 20/177 (11.3%)
of patients, compared to 11.1 expected under independence (1.81-fold
enrichment, Fisher exact p<0.05). This is consistent with the model's
predicted terminal state in which both NK and CD8+ compartments reach
zero.

(2) NK low correlates with reduced TIM-3 expression. Patients with
low NCAM1 showed lower HAVCR2/TIM-3 expression (r=-0.185, p=0.013),
consistent with overall immune compartment depletion. In NK-low patients,
mean CD8A z-score was -0.370 vs +0.186 in NK-high patients.

(3) CD8A-IFNG functional axis preserved. CD8A correlated positively
with IFNG (r=+0.360, p<0.001), confirming the functional link between
CD8+ T cell abundance and IFN-γ production modeled in OncoBiome Swarm.

Two model predictions were not confirmed in bulk RNA-seq data:
IL6 vs IFNG (r=+0.060, NS) and NCAM1 vs CD8A overall (r=-0.067, NS),
likely due to stromal confounding in bulk tumor specimens — a known
limitation of bulk RNA-seq for immune cell quantification in desmoplastic
PDAC.

We note that scRNA-seq data from Werba et al. (GSE212966, n=27) with
cell-type-resolved NK and CD8+ subpopulations would provide a more
direct test; this analysis is planned as a future extension.

TEXTO (Limitaciones — añadir):
"External consistency analysis used bulk RNA-seq data (TCGA-PAAD n=177),
which cannot resolve immune cell proportions from stromal admixture nor
capture temporal dynamics. These analyses test terminal-state consistency
only. Cell-type-resolved scRNA-seq validation (e.g., GSE212966) is
a planned future extension."
